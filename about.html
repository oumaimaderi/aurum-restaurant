OUR STORY

SINCE 2010

The Visionary

Chef Marcus Bennett

With over two decades of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Chef Marcus Bennett brought his vision of culinary perfection to life when he opened AURUM in 2010.

His philosophy is simple: let the ingredients speak. By forging relationships with the world's finest purveyors and farmers, Marcus ensures that every dish tells a story of place, season, and passion.

"Cooking is not just about nourishment—it's about creating memories," says Marcus. "At AURUM, we invite you to be part of our story."
